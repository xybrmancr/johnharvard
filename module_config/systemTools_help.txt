Bot Commands
------------
Help:
 - '!help', '!cmd' - Triggers this message!

Text:
 - '!shruggie' - ¯\_(ツ)_/¯

Memes:
 - '!bot_avatar' - Eclectus Bot's avatar!


Games:
 - '!roll', '!roll(%d&)' - Rolls dice, where %=number of dice, and &=number of sides (default=1d6)
 - '!flip', '!coin' - Flips a fair, 2 sided coin
 - '!rng' - Generates a random number from 0-100, inclusive

Magic 8 Ball:
 - '!magic', '!magic8', '!m8' - Ask the Magic 8 Ball a question!
